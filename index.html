Harry Potter House Mascots

By Caitlin Ostroff

For those of you obsessed with Harry Potter, you probably know your House and mascot. If you're like me and your letter got lost in the mail, you can still interact with your house mascot just like magic. Click on the picture of your mascot and see what happens!

Hufflepuff, whose mascot is the badger, represents hard work and loyalty.

Ravenclaw, whose mascot is the eagle, represents wisdom.

Gryffindor, whose mascot is the lion, represents bravery.

Slytherin, whose mascot is the snake, represents ambition.

Badger image from Clip Art Lord. Eagle, lion and snake images from ClipArtix. Castle sky background from All-Free-Download free stock photos.
